Algarve The Algarve, a region in the far south of Portugal, is known for its Mediterranean beaches and golf resorts. Its Mediterranean cuisine is also known throughout the world for the best and tastiest gastronomy. It's based on olive oil, grilled meats and fresh fish, caught on the day.
